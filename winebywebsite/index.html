Why does this exist?

a brief history

Following the repeal of Prohibition, PA created the Pennsylvania Liquor Control Board to regulate the sale of alcoholic beverages. According to Wikipedia, the purpose of the board was to, "discourage the purchase of alcoholic beverages by making it as inconvenient and expensive as possible."

That ethos continues to today. While Wine & Spirits runs monthly and weekly sales, it is not possible to search sales by store. That's where we come in. For those of us who are both lazy and cheap, we aggregate the monthly specials for $25 or less available in stores near you, and send them to you in a monthly roundup email. Actual availability may change, YMMV.
